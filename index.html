Lesson 1: What Is AI?

What Is AI? AI stands for Artificial Intelligence – a computer that can “think” in some ways, like solving problems, recognizing patterns, or making decisions.

Where Do We See AI?

  • Maths: Online calculators that show steps
  • Science: Weather models, lab robots
  • Languages: Google Translate, grammar checkers
Activity: Find 5 things (apps, sites, devices) that use AI. Write what they do and how they might use AI.

Lesson 2: Meet Your AI Study Buddy

Smart Prompts: Ask clear, focused questions.

  • Math: Step-by-step help, quizzes
  • Science: Concept explanations, summaries
  • Languages: Vocabulary, grammar fixes, story writing
Quiz: Which is a good AI question?
Tell me about science.
Explain how volcanoes form like I'm 12. ✅
Activity: Ask AI about a science topic or get help with a story idea.

Write a story about a robot who gets lost on Mars.

Lesson 3: Creative AI – Art, Music, Stories

  • Story writing with AI help
  • AI-generated art
  • Music with Chrome Music Lab
Activity: Write a story or create a "Math Monster" character.

Lesson 4: Be Smart with Smart Tech

AI Can: Explain, help, translate.
AI Can't: Always be right or understand emotions.

  • Double-check important info
  • Don’t share personal details
  • Use AI to learn, not cheat
Scenario: AI gives you a wrong answer. What do you do?

Lesson 5: Your First AI Project

  • Math: Create a quiz with AI-generated questions
  • Science: Make a study guide with AI help
  • Languages: Translate a story with AI
Final Task: Choose one project idea and write your reflections.
